Domestic Shipping

All domestic orders process within 24-48 hours. We partner with specialized couriers who maintain proper temperature control for fragrance integrity.

Signature required for all 100ml bottles and special items.

International Shipping

We ship internationally via express courier. Rates calculated based on weight and destination at checkout.

Some fragrances may be subject to regional regulations. Our team will contact you if restrictions apply.

Returns & Exchanges

Every purchase includes a 2ml sample. If unsatisfied, return the unopened full-size bottle within 30 days for a refund.

Opened fragrances cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons. Custom and limited editions are final sale.

How to Return

1. Start a return request online below
2. Pack the unopened item in original packaging
3. Affix the prepaid label
4. Refund processes in 5-10 business days
